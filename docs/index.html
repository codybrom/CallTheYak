Call the Yak

A faithful recreation of the hidden yak easter egg from Final Cut Pro. Bruce the Wonder Yak trots across the bottom of your screen and shares pearls of wisdom.

Download for Mac

v1.0.0  ·  macOS 14 Sonoma or later  ·  All releases

Installing

  1. Download and unzip CallTheYak.zip
  2. Drag the app to your Applications folder
  3. Launch it and Call the Yak from your menu bar
