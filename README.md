# Call the Yak

<img src="bruce.svg" width="96" alt="Bruce the Wonder Yak" />

A faithful modern macOS recreation of an old Final Cut Pro easter egg.

Bruce is a brown yak who lives in your Mac. Every so often he trots across the bottom of your screen, stops to graze and shares pearls of wisdom via thought bubble. But don't get too close or you might scare him off.

## Installing

Unzip the release, then drag `Call the Yak.app` to your Applications folder.

## How It Works

Bruce runs in your **menu bar** with options to:

- **Call the Yak** (or press **Cmd+Shift+Y**) to summon Bruce immediately
- **Settings** to adjust how often he appears (5 min to 2 hours)
- **Quit Bruce**, when you've had enough yak

When Bruce appears:

1. A grass patch poofs into existence on your screen
2. Bruce trots in from the right
3. He stops, grazes, and shares a random musings in a thought bubble
4. He'll loops through his library of quotes until you move your mouse near him, at which point he'll bolt off-screen

## Building

Requires **Xcode 15+** and **macOS 14.0 (Sonoma)** or later.
